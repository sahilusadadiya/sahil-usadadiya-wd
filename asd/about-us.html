Our Story

Meet Anchal Saraf Devnani

The journey of Rooted by ASD began with a passion for natural beauty and craftsmanship. Anchal Saraf Devnani, our founder, discovered her love for wooden artistry while seeking unique pieces for her own home.

What started as a personal appreciation for the warmth and character of wood quickly evolved into a mission to create premium handcrafted wooden products that bring nature's elegance into homes.

Each piece created under Anchal's guidance celebrates the unique grain patterns and natural beauty that only wood can offer, telling a story of craftsmanship and dedication.

Rooted captures the soul of timeless design and the warmth of natural craftsmanship.

To create timeless wooden pieces that bring nature's beauty into your home, while supporting sustainable practices and traditional craftsmanship.

Quality

We use only premium quality wood, sourced responsibly, and finished with natural oils to enhance its inherent beauty.

Craftsmanship

Each product is handcrafted with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring uniqueness and character in every piece.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable practices, ensuring our production process respects the environment from which our materials come.

Our Materials

To create timeless wooden pieces that bring nature's beauty into your home, while supporting sustainable practices and traditional craftsmanship.

Sheesham Wood

Known for its rich, golden to deep brown color and distinctive grain pattern. Shesham is highly valued for its durability and natural luster, making it perfect for premium wooden products.

Saag Wood

Saag wood is valued for its natural texture and warm tones. It's slightly lighter in color than Sheesham but equally strong and beautiful, making it perfect for our handcrafted pieces.
